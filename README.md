Customer Churn Prediction & Retention Analytics

Project Overview

Customer churn is a critical business challenge that directly impacts revenue and profitability. This project analyzes customer behavior, identifies churn drivers, segments customers, and develops predictive machine learning models to identify customers at risk of leaving.



Business Objectives

- Understand customer churn behavior
- Identify key churn drivers
- Segment customers based on tenure
- Build predictive churn models
- Generate actionable retention recommendations



Dataset

Telco Customer Churn Dataset

- 7043 customer records
- 21 original features
- 31 engineered features after preprocessing

Key Findings

- Tenure is the strongest predictor of churn.
- Month-to-month contracts drive higher churn.
- Higher monthly charges increase churn risk.
- Fiber optic customers exhibit elevated churn behavior.
- Automatic payment methods improve retention.



Business Recommendations

- Improve onboarding for new customers.
- Incentivize long-term contracts.
- Reduce churn among high-billing customers.
- Promote automatic payment methods.
- Develop proactive churn prevention strategies.
